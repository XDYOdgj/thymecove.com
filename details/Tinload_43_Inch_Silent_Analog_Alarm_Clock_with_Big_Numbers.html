Tinload 4.3 Inch Silent Analog Alarm Clock

Designed for a peaceful night's sleep, this silent analog alarm clock features no ticking sound to avoid waking you up unnecessarily. With large, easy-to-read numbers on a white dial, this clock is perfect for seniors, children, and anyone with poor vision.
